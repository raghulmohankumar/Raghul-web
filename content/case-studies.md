**Electrical System Enhancement – Mining Sensor Platform** – MineSense Technologies Ltd
- Integration of new electrical modules into an existing mining sensor platform while maintaining safety, reliability, and manufacturability.
- Contributions: Led electrical change management, updated control panel schematics, wiring harnesses, and BOMs, supported risk assessment, validation, and documentation.
- Outcome: Improved system robustness and production readiness.

**Electrical System Development & Modernization – Elevator Platforms** – KONE Corporation
- Development and modernization of elevator electrical systems to support new features, regulatory compliance, and cost optimization.
- Contributions: Designed electrical circuits, harnesses, and emergency systems; performed system-level electrical analysis using ETAP; led validation, supplier coordination, and field support.
- Outcome: Delivered compliant, manufacturable, and reliable designs.
